# 🎮 Gtanks
A 2D tank game played in the browser, for 1 to 3 players on the same keyboard. Take on a bot or fight your friends on various maps with obstacles, power-ups and special weapons.

# 🕹️ Game Modes
1 Player – fight against a bot with 3 difficulty levels: Easy, Hard, Extreme
2 Player – duel between two players
3 Player – three-way battle on the same map

# 🗺️ Maps
The game includes 6 maps that rotate automatically each round:
Empty, Classic, Maze, Fortress, Maze 2, Lines.

# 🎯 Power-ups (multiplayer only)
Mine – place a mine on the ground
Rocket – launch a self-guided rocket
Remote Rocket – control the rocket manually
Laser – fire an instant laser beam
Wallbreaker – bullet destroys walls

# ⌨️ Default Controls
Green: W / A / S / D to move, E to shoot
Blue: Arrow keys to move, M to shoot
Red: Y / G / H / J to move, U to shoot

Controls can be reconfigured from the ready screen.

# ⚙️ Settings
Tank Speed – tank movement speed (0.5x – 2x)
Bullet Speed – bullet speed (1x – 5x)
Self-Destruct – enables being hit by your own bullet
Power-ups – toggle power-ups on/off
